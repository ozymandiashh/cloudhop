Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

CloudHop wraps rclone in a clean interface so you can focus on moving files, not reading docs.

🔒

Fully Private

No accounts, no telemetry, no tracking. Your credentials stay on your machine and never touch our servers.

⚡

Parallel Transfers

Multi-threaded transfers saturate your connection. Move terabytes in hours, not days.

🌐

70+ Providers

S3, Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, Backblaze B2, Azure, SFTP, and dozens more out of the box.

🔄

Real-time Sync

Bi-directional sync watches for changes and keeps your clouds in perfect mirror.

📦

Built on rclone

Battle-tested rclone engine under the hood. If rclone supports it, CloudHop supports it.

🎨

Beautiful UI

A native desktop app that feels right at home on macOS, Windows, and Linux.

How it works

Three steps, zero friction

No sign-ups, no cloud middlemen. Just download and go.

01

Install CloudHop

Download the app or install via pip. CloudHop bundles rclone automatically -- nothing else to configure.

02

Connect your clouds

Add any provider with a guided setup. OAuth flows open in your browser; tokens are stored locally, encrypted.

03

Move or sync

Pick a source and destination, hit start. CloudHop handles retries, checksums, and bandwidth throttling.
